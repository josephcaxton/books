# Product Vision: The AI-Native Executive

## Core Objective
To write an authoritative, forward-looking strategic playbook for corporate leaders shifting their enterprises from AI assistants to autonomous agentic infrastructure.

## Target Audience Themes
- Primary: Fortune 500 C-Suite (CEO, COO, CFO), Mid-market founders, and Board Members.
- Secondary: VPs of Operations, Enterprise Architects, and Management Consultants.

## Non-Goals (What NOT to write)
- DO NOT write coding tutorials, Python scripts, or API setup guides.
- DO NOT use basic, outdated 2023 fluff (e.g., "How to write a prompt for marketing"). 
- Avoid generic academic ethical lectures; focus instead on functional enterprise risk management.
