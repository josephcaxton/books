# About the Author

**Joseph Caxton-Idowu** is a Forward Deployed AI and Cloud Engineer with over twenty years of experience in IT, delivering enterprise-grade cloud transformation across financial services, media, retail, and technology.

Born and raised in Nigeria, Joseph moved to the United Kingdom to build a self determined future — a journey that forms the backbone of this book. He began his career in technology after an early encounter with a DOS computer sparked what he immediately recognised as his life purpose. That moment, and the spiritual awakening that followed it, set the course for everything that came next.

Over the course of his career, Joseph has worked with some of the most recognised organisations in the UK and beyond — including Barclaycard, Avanade, eBay Kleinanzeigen, Deloitte, Barclays Bank, BBC, Aviva, TfL, Sky, Bauer Media, and Provident Financial. He is the founder of **First Cloud Solutions**, through which he brings enterprise-level cloud and AI engineering directly to mid-size and large organisations. He is also the founder of **LearnersCloud** ([learnerscloud.com](https://learnerscloud.com)), a video-on-demand GCSE and iGCSE learning platform used by students all over the world.

His technical expertise spans the full breadth of cloud engineering: large-scale AWS migrations, Kubernetes platform design, cloud security transformation, DevOps delivery, and — most recently — forward deployed AI engineering. He holds five AWS certifications, including the DevOps Engineer Professional, Solutions Architect Professional, Security Specialty, Advanced Networking Specialty, and AI Practitioner.

Beyond the technical work, Joseph is a mentor — actively investing in the next generation of professionals and sharing the hard-won lessons that fill these pages.
