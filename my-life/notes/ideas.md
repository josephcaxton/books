# Book Notes & Ideas

## Title (Confirmed)

**The Price I Paid: A Father's Wisdom for the Next Generation**

### Why This Title Works
- "The Price I Paid" — every scar, every failed business, every hard lesson in the book is the price.
- "A Father's Wisdom for the Next Generation" — signals exactly who the book is for and positions it as a legacy memoir, not a generic self-help book.

## Core Themes

- The Two Teachers: Instruction vs. Experience
- Purpose must be discovered AND built — it cannot be handed to you
- Spiritual alignment is a prerequisite for building anything lasting
- Energy, order, and attention to detail are the foundations of quality
- Time is the only truly finite resource
- Run your own race — trends are not callings
- The best you can be requires lifelong deliberate learning
- Legacy is wisdom transferred before it is too late

## Key Aphorisms (Book-Level)

- "Experience gives the test first and the lesson afterward."
- "You cannot plant thorns and expect to harvest grapes."
- "I want you to have my wisdom without having to pay my price."
- "Clarity is not a gift handed to the passive. It is a reward earned by the moving."
- "Capital without knowledge is not an advantage. It is a faster way to lose more."
- "The beginning is not a financial position. It is an educational one."
- "Guard your time. It is the only thing that, once gone, cannot be returned to you at any price."
