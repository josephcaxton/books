# Kiro Steering: Character Profiles

## The Author (Protagonist)
*   **Role:** The Guide, Father/Ancestor, and Student of Experience.
*   **Motivations:** To pass down wisdom, save his descendants from unnecessary suffering, and transform his past pain into ancestral instructions.
*   **Relationships:** 
    *   *With Parents:* Tension between respecting their high expectations and suffering under their forced choices.
    *   *With Descendants:* Deeply protective, loving, urgent, and transparent.
*   **Character Arc:** Moves from a passive, auto-pilot youth crushed by external expectations into a reckless period of rebellion, culminating in a dual technical and spiritual awakening that grants him agency as the architect of his own destiny.

## The Parents
*   **Role:** Well-meaning Catalyst of Friction.
*   **Motivations:** Providing the absolute best future, status, and elite education for their child.
*   **Relationships:** 
    *   *With The Author:* Loving but top-down; they push plans (elite school, military, studying abroad) based on external metrics rather than the author's internal identity.
*   **Character Arc:** Static forces of tradition and protection whose well-intentioned pressures inadvertently trigger the protagonist's "wilderness" phases.

## The Two Teachers (Conceptual Characters)
*   **Role:** The Dual Antagonist and Mentor of the overarching narrative.
*   **Motivations:** 
    *   *Instruction:* To give wisdom freely to those humble enough to listen before getting burned.
    *   *Experience:* To extract payment in tears, time, and money by giving the test first and the lesson afterward.
*   **Relationships:** Both compete for the mind, choices, and direction of the Author and his future generations.

## The Darkness (Antagonist / Internal Force)
*   **Role:** Psychological and Spiritual Oppression.
*   **Motivations:** To keep the Author blind, operating on autopilot, and trapped in a wild, destructive lifestyle.
*   **Relationships:** Attaches to the Author during the transition to the elite secondary school and detaches instantly during the church revival.
*   **Character Arc:** Grows from a cloud of lost confidence into a wild storm of self-destruction, before being entirely dissolved by spiritual light.
