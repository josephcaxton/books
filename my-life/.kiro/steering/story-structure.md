# Kiro Steering: Story Structure

## Structural Framework
*   **The Hybrid Legacy Architecture:** A deliberate blend of chronological memoir (the story) and actionable self-help (the instruction).
*   **The Narrative Blueprint:** Every major phase of the book must strictly follow a three-part progression:
    1.  *The Scar (The Catalyst):* A vulnerable account of a mistake, forced path, or failure.
    2.  *The Extracted Tool (The Turning Point):* The specific lesson, technical skill, or spiritual awakening born from that event.
    3.  *The Blueprint (The Legacy):* Direct, concise instructions transferring the wisdom to the next generation.

## Macro-Architecture (The Core Narrative Arc)

### Act I: The Auto-Pilot & The Forced Path
*   **Focus:** Establishing the "Two Teachers" philosophy and the dangers of external blueprints.
*   **Key Milestones:**
    *   The illusion of the easy path versus the cost of experience.
    *   Transitioning from a natural, high-confidence local environment to a high-pressure elite school.
    *   The birth of "The Darkness" and the subsequent loss of personal direction.

### Act II: The Wilderness & The Tools
*   **Focus:** Character extraction through unexpected or incomplete journeys.
*   **Key Milestones:**
    *   The prophetic warning (The Stadium Dream) and entering the military academy.
    *   The "incomplete" path that nevertheless yields vital tools: discipline, tenacity, and courage.
    *   The post-military slide into reckless, destructive lifestyles (partying, distraction).

### Act III: The Dual Awakening & The Foundation
*   **Focus:** Liberation, alignment, and preparing the legacy.
*   **Key Milestones:**
    *   The Technical Catalyst: Discovering the DOS computer and sparking a life purpose.
    *   The Spiritual Catalyst: Obeying the intuitive whisper to attend the revival, leading to instant deliverance from the darkness.
    *   The Launch: Transitioning abroad with a clear mind to build a self-determined destiny.

## Micro-Chapter Anatomy (The Rule of 3)
To honor the author's mandate for a short, concise, and highly readable book, every standard chapter must be rigidly structured into exactly three sub-sections:
*   **Section 1: The Lesson I Was Forced to Learn** (The conceptual or philosophical warning).
*   **Section 2: The Stories of My Journey** (The chronological personal narrative/anecdotes).
*   **Section 3: My Advice to the Next Generation** (The scannable, punchy summary of instructions).
