# Kiro Steering: Worldbuilding

## Locations
*   **The Primary School Environment:** A local, nurturing school close to home where the Author naturally excelled, felt safe, and maintained high confidence.
*   **The Elite Secondary School:** A high-pressure, prestigious institution in town that felt heavy, alienating, and acted as the birthplace of "The Darkness."
*   **The Military Academy:** A stark, regimented, and intense environment that served as a wilderness of self-discovery, building raw character despite being left incomplete.
*   **The Local Computer School:** A newly opened storefront or classroom in town, representing a portal to the future, technical enlightenment, and personal purpose.
*   **The Pentecostal Revival Hall:** A local church venue filled with energy, music, and spiritual fervor, serving as the physical site of the Author's liberation.

## Rules of the World
*   **The Law of the Harvest:** A fixed, non-negotiable universal principle stating that you inevitably reap exactly what you sow; autopilot living never leads to intentional destinations.
*   **The Rule of Two Teachers:** All human education is strictly split into Instruction (the protective shortcut) and Experience (the brutal, expensive truth).
*   **The Cost of Wisdom:** Lessons from experience must always be paid for upfront using the world's three hardest currencies: tears, time, or money.
*   **The Blueprint Mandate:** A rule stating that purpose cannot be given or inherited; a person must actively discovery and physically build their own foundation.
*   **Spiritual Intuition:** The law that God speaks via quiet internal whispers and vivid symbolic dreams, requiring immediate obedience to avoid destruction.

## Historical Events
*   **The Elite Transition:** The forced relocation of the Author from a thriving local school to an elite academy, which derailed his academic path.
*   **The Prophetic Stadium Dream:** A supernatural event where a dream of a half-built fence and missing tires accurately predicted the Author's upcoming military departure.
*   **The Technical Spark:** The Author's historic first encounter with a standalone computer, permanently changing his career trajectory.
*   **The Spiritual Awakening:** The evening revival meeting that instantly lifted a multi-year psychological cloud and initiated a fresh start.

## Technology & Magic Systems
*   **The DOS Operating System:** The early, text-based computer technology that acted as the catalyst for the Author's professional destiny.
*   **Divine Discernment / Dream Interpretation:** The narrative's supernatural mechanism, where spiritual symbols are sent to warn or direct the protagonist.
*   **Spiritual Deliverance:** The unseen mechanism where choosing faith instantly breaks psychological oppression ("The Darkness") and restores mental clarity.
